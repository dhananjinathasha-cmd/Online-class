About Thakshi Fashions

Welcome to Thakshi Fashions, your creative sanctuary for learning the beautiful art of handmade crafts and fashion design. Founded with a passion for creativity and teaching, we specialize in helping beginners and advanced learners master the skills of crochet, resin art, and fashion accessory making.

Our comprehensive courses cover everything from crochet bags and trendy crochet tops to stunning resin art pieces and unique handmade fashion accessories. Whether you're looking to start a new hobby or build a handmade business, Thakshi Fashions provides the guidance, techniques, and inspiration you need.
